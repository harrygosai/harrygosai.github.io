District Eco Club School Program
Overview

The District Eco Club School Program encouraged schools across Bhavnagar district to take part in nature walks, interactive sessions, and live demonstrations. Students learned about trees, water conservation, birds, reptiles, and simple actions to protect biodiversity.

  • Interactive sessions, guided trails, and nature-art activities.
  • Emphasis on local wildlife recognition and hands-on conservation.
  • Students received small plant saplings to care for at home/school.
Key theme: Empowering young nature ambassadors through practical learning.

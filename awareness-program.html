Introduction

The Snake and Bird Awareness Program was started in Bhavnagar/Ahmedabad to educate the public and students about the importance of snakes and birds in our ecosystem. The main aim is to remove fear and myths about snakes, teach safe methods, and promote kindness towards birds ‚Äî especially during kite flying and summer seasons.

Local rescuers, forest officers, and volunteers conduct small sessions in schools, colleges, and residential areas, spreading the message of compassion and coexistence.

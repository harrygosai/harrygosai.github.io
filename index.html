#Welcome

Experience the Wild Through the Eyes of Harry Gosai!

Since 2015, Harry Gosai has dedicated his life to wildlife rescue, conservation, and photography. From rescuing over 10,000+ animals and birds to conducting awareness programs, his mission is simple — to coexist peacefully with nature and inspire others to do the same.

Government-Authorized Wildlife Rescuer
Member of ERN (Emergency Relief Network) under WTI & IFAW
Collaborates with the Forest Department of Gujarat and NGOs
Passionate Wildlife Photographer & Educator
